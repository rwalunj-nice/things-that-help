## Action item rules

### 1. Direct name mention required
Only classify an item as an action item if Rahul is explicitly @mentioned by name
("Rahul" or "Rahul Walunj") in the message body.
- Group channel mentions (@Cloud Operations, @WFM Team, etc.) do NOT qualify.
- Being a member of a mentioned group does NOT qualify.

### 2. Team approval / change requests via group channels — ignore
CHG tickets, CAB approvals, or any approval requests posted in group channels are NOT
action items for Rahul. Only surface these if someone pings Rahul directly 1-1 and
asks for his specific approval.

### 3. Incident / P2 / Major Incident Teams notifications — ignore
P2, Major Incident, or large-scale incident notifications that arrive as Teams
missed-activity emails (sender: 365-noreply@nice.com) are NOT action items.
Only surface incidents if Rahul receives a direct email (not a Teams redirect).

### 4. Ask must be on Rahul, not on others
If a message names Rahul in context but the ask is directed at other people, it is
NOT an action item for Rahul. The item is only actionable when Rahul is the one
expected to respond or act.
