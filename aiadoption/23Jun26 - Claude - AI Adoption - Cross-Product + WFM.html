Top performers (90%+)

IEX Core peaked at 97% in PI2.S2 — the highest single-sprint AI usage recorded across all products this PI. ESP maintains a rock-solid 89-95% band with no dips, suggesting mature AI-first culture in Ninja/Thundercats teams.

Stable mid-range

CXone Integrations (WFM Integrations) shows a remarkably flat 74-77% band across S1-S3, dropping slightly to 63% planned in S192. CXone WFM improved from 75% → 80% S1→S2 with the largest epic portfolio (109 AI-tagged epics).

S192 signal

All products are in early S192 delivery. IEX Core leads at 87% planned; ESP holds 95% planned. CXone Integrations shows lower planned AI rate (63%) suggesting more non-AI work in this sprint cycle. Delivery rates remain low (5-14%) — expect final numbers by 1 Jul.

Epic coverage vs sprint delivery

IEX Core has highest epic AI% (93%) with the tightest delivery at 32% closed. CXone WFM leads epic closure at 46% (74 of 162). ESP has the best epic delivery rate at 43% despite fewer absolute epics — suggesting smaller, faster-closing epics.
